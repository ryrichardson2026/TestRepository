FAQ

Is this an exact quote?
No. This gives you a realistic local range based on homes and installs similar to yours.
How do you build your pricing estimates?
Our pricing ranges are built from contractor quotes, pricing conversations shared on social media, and research from our local pricing team. The goal is to give homeowners a realistic starting point before they compare local quotes.
Why can quotes vary so much?
System size, layout, ductwork, labor, and installer approach can change the total by thousands.
Will I need to talk to someone right away?
No. You can get your estimate first. Speaking with a contractor is optional.
Should I still compare quotes?
Yes. This helps you understand what looks reasonable so you can compare quotes with more confidence.
